Quick Answers
Will my data be saved if I close the tab?
▼
Yes! Everything is saved automatically in your browser's Local Storage. Close the tab, come back later — your subjects and tasks will still be there waiting for you.
Does it work on my phone?
▼
Absolutely! StudyPlan is fully responsive. Whether you're on a phone, tablet, or laptop — it adapts to look great on any screen size.
Is StudyPlan really free?
▼
100% free, always! No accounts to create, no subscriptions, no hidden fees. We built this to help students — not to make money from them.
How do I delete all my data?
▼
Open your browser's Developer Tools (F12), go to Application → Local Storage, and clear the entries for this site. Or just open a new browser and start fresh!
