Study Tips

Boost Your Productivity

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break. After 4 cycles, take a longer break of 15–30 minutes. This rhythm keeps your brain fresh and focused.

Active Recall

Don't just re-read notes. Close them and try to recall the key concepts. Testing yourself is far more effective than passive reading for long-term retention.

Set SMART Goals

Make your study goals Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound. Use your StudyPlan countdown timers to stay on track every single day.
